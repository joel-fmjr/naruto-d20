# Ninja Chef

## Requirements

To qualify to become a ninja chef, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +2
*   **Skills**: Craft (Cuisine) 6 Ranks, Fuinjutsu 6 ranks, Chakra Control 6 ranks
*   **Feats**: Chakra Weaving, Sealweaver

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Ninja Chef prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Ninja Chef gains 1d6 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Ninja Chef gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, everytime he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: Concentration (Con), Chakra Control (Wis), Craft (Cuisine) (Int), Fuinjutsu (Int), Gather Information (Cha), Investigate (Int), Listen (Wis), Perform (Cooking Demo) (Cha), Research (Int), Search (Int), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Treat Injury (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 6 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Ninja Chef prestige class.

### Chakra Infusion (Su)
Chakra Infusions remain stable for 10 minutes, after which the sealed chakra is released and the food becomes an ordinary, if savory dish.

*   **Chakra Restoration**: Food created by the Ninja Chef restores 10 points of Chakra. Can be empowered to increase Chakra restored by 5 points per extra Meta-Chakra Charge.
*   **Health Restoration**: Food created by the Ninja Chef restores 10 points of Health. Can be empowered to increase Health restored by 5 points per extra Meta-Chakra Charge.
*   **Skill Enhancement**: Food created by the Ninja Chef enhances a ninja's abilities. Choose either Ninjutsu, Taijutsu, Genjutsu or Chakra Control. The chosen skill gains a +4 enhancement bonus. Can be empowered to increase bonus by +2 per extra Meta-Chakra Charge. Can be taken multiple times, each time for a different skill. Requires one other infusion.
*   **Defense Enhancement**: Food created by the Ninja Chef enhances a ninja's defenses. Choose either Reflex, Will or Fortitude. The chosen save gains a +4 enhancement bonus. Can be empowered to increase bonus by +2 per extra MetaChakra Charge. Can be taken multiple times, each time for a different skill. Requires one other infusion.
*   **Physical Prowess**: Food created by the Ninja Chef increases physical prowess. Choose either Strength, Dexterity or Constitution. The chosen ability gains a +4 enhancement bonus for 5 rounds upon consumption. Can be empowered to increase duration by 5 rounds per extra Meta-Chakra Charge. Requires two other infusions.
*   **Mental Prowess**: Food created by the Ninja Chef increases mental prowess. Choose either Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma. The chosen ability gains a +4 enhancement bonus for 5 rounds upon consumption. Can be empowered to increase duration by 5 rounds per extra Meta-Chakra Charge. Requires two other infusions.

### Iron Gut (Ex)
The Ninja Chef's innards grow more hardened to outside interference. The Ninja Chef gains a bonus to saving throws vs. Poisons and Nausea Effects targeting Fortitude. This bonus increases with level to a maximum of +10.

### Soul Food (Su)
The Ninja Chef's culinary skills give her meals a special quality all their own. Those who consume a meal with a Chakra Infusion prepared by the Ninja Chef gain a morale bonus to all attack rolls and saves for 10 minutes afterward. This bonus increases with level to a maximum of +5.

### Sneak Attack
The Ninja Chef has learned to apply her knowledge of anatomy and choice cuts of meat to her attacks and gains a Sneak Attack.

### Bonus Feats
At 4th and 8th level, the Ninja Chef gets a bonus feat. The bonus feat must be selected from the following list, and the Ninja Chef must meet all the prerequisites of the feat to select it.
*   Culinary Prowess
