# Dao Shi Monk

The Dao Shi Monk is a rarity in the ninja world who focuses his attention on practicing the martial art of Dao Shi, a fluid style of defensive martial arts that combines the strength of Taijutsu with advanced chakra control manipulation. This style has been passed down from generation to generation within the Myugan community.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a dao shi monk, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +3
*   **Skills**: Chakra Control 9 ranks, Taijutsu 9 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Defensive Martial Arts, Harmony, Taijutsu Adept.
*   **Special**: Must have 3 Stance Taijutsu techniques.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Dao Shi Monk prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: d8
*   **Action Points**: 7 + ½ character level, rounded down.
*   **Class Skills**: Balance (Dex), Bluff (Cha), Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Escape Artist (Dex), Listen (Wis), Profession (Wis), Sense Motive (Wis), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Dao Shi Monk prestige class.

### Dao Shi Style

This defensive style of fighting involves a high level of perception, anticipation, and overall skill. Those who learn this special martial art soon become renowned for their patience and precision. When the Dao Shi Monk is fighting unarmed, he may fight defensively without suffering a penalty to attack rolls (This cannot be used in conjunction with the Combat Expertise feat). In addition, any time a creature enters the Dao Shi Monk’s threat range, he may take an attack of opportunity at his highest attack bonus. He may not take more than one attack of opportunity per round (unless he has taken the Combat Reflexes feat).

### Stance Mastery

The Dao Shi monk quickly becomes a master of Taijutsu Stances, and uses them frequently to lure enemies in before striking with devastating precision. The character may learn Stance Taijutsu techniques that are 2 ranks higher than his level, and gains a +1 bonus to learn and perform checks.

### Chakra Control Mastery

The Dao Shi Monk gains a natural affinity for chakra control and becomes a master of techniques that utilize it. The character may learn Chakra Control techniques that are 2 ranks higher than his level, and gains a +1 bonus to learn and perform checks.
