# Space-Time Specialist

The space-time specialist is a shinobi who is adept in the ways of space-time Ninjutsu.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a space-time specialist, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Ninjutsu 13 ranks, Knowledge (Ninja Lore) 12 ranks, Concentration 10 Ranks.
*   **Feats**: Retrieval Expert, Ninjutsu Adept.
*   **Special**: Must know at least 3 Space-Time techniques.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Space-Time Specialist prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: d8
*   **Action Points**: 7 + ½ character level, rounded down.
*   **Class Skills**: Balance (Dex), Bluff (Cha), Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Craft (Calligraphy, Mechanical) (Int), Escape Artist (Dex), Hide (Dex), Jump (Str), Move Silently (Dex), Ninjutsu (Int), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Survival (Wis), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Space-Time Specialist prestige class.

### Space-Time Adept

You gain a +2 bonus to Ninjutsu checks to perform or identify techniques with the Space-Time descriptor and the DCs of your Space-Time techniques are increased by 1. In addition, you can make 1 extra attempt when learning Space-Time Ninjutsu techniques. You gain a +1 bonus to your effective skill threshold when performing Space-Time Ninjutsu techniques.

### Teleportation

Twice per day the character may teleport as per Jigen Ugoku no Jutsu, except the user may not transport another creature with him. The character does not have to know the technique in order to use this ability. At 4th level in this class, the number of times this ability can be used increases to 4 times per day. At 5th level in this class the user may transport one other creature. This ability functions as a move action.

### Space-Time Avoidance

Avoiding an Attack: The character may use this ability as an instant action before an enemy’s attack roll (melee or ranged attack, touch attack, or grapple attempt) targeting him specifically, or before he is hit by a technique that allows a reflex save or effect that provokes an attack roll, as per Kawarimi and Tobikiri Kawarimi no Jutsu. The character can still use this ability if he is immobilized or flat-footed, so long as he is aware of the enemy. To avoid the attack, the user must make a Reflex save against the attack roll (add 5 if the attack is a natural 20 or confirmed critical and an additional 5 if the character is immobilized or flat-footed) or technique save DC (add 5 if the character is immobilized or flatfooted). Success means the character avoids the attack and vanishes from sight with no visual or auditory cue, and reappears in a chosen location within the character’s base land speed (halved if previously immobilized). This ability can only be used once per encounter and costs 3 points of chakra.

### Space-Time Mastery

The Space-Time Specialist has become a master of Space-Time manipulation, able to perform Space-Time techniques as if they were second nature. A number of times per day equal to his Intelligence modifier, the character can choose one of the following effects:

**Space-Time Extension:** The character can triple the range of a Space-Time technique with the teleportation descriptor.

**Cost Reduction:** The character can reduce the chakra cost of a Space-Time technique by half (rounded up).

**Quickened Space-Time Technique:** The character gains a quickened action to perform a single Space-Time technique with a perform time no longer than 1 attack action. The Space-Time Specialist can only gain one quickened action per round, no matter the type (See Basic Game Mechanics for rules on Quickened Actions).

### Bonus Feat

Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Acrobatic, Agile Riposte, Armor Proficiency, Athletic, Chuunin, Combat Expertise, Combat Martial Arts, Combat Reflexes, Combat Tactics, Defensive Martial Arts, Dodge, Elusive Target, Improved Avoidance, Improved Chakra Pool, Improved Combat Expertise, Improved Disarm, Improved Feint, Improved Initiative, Improved Trip, Jounin, Lightning Reflexes, Mobility, Stealthy, Taijutsu Adept, Talented Shinobi.
