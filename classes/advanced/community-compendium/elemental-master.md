# Elemental Master

The Elemental Master is an authority on elemental jutsus. Their prowess enables them to warp and twist their jutsu in new and bizarre manners. The fastest path to this class is through the Smart Hero basic class.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a elemental master, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Ninjutsu 15 ranks, Chakra Control 15 ranks
*   **Feats**: Ninjutsu Adept
*   **Special**: You may only take levels in your Affinity. Must know at least 7 Ninjutsu techniques of their Affinity.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Elemental Master prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: d6
*   **Action Points**: 7 + ½ character levels, rounded down.
*   **Class Skills**: Balance (Dex), Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Craft (chemical) (Int), Hide (Dex), Knowledge (current events, history, ninja lore, physical science, popular culture) (Int), Listen (Wis), Move Silently (Dex), Ninjutsu (Int), Profession (Wis), Read Language (None), Search (Int), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Elemental Master prestige class.

### Elemental Amplification

At first level, the Elemental Master’s Affinity becomes more powerful, all jutsu that have the same type as his Affinity receive an additional +1 damage per die of damage. This bonus increases to +2 at level 5.

### Talents

Choose one of the following Talents. A Talent cannot be chosen twice unless specified.

### Advanced Ninjutsu Talent Tree

**Smart Ninjutsu (Requires Ninjutsu talent):** You may reroll any Ninjutsu check, but you have to keep the rerolled result, even if it is worse.

**Practiced Ninjutsu (Requires Smart Ninjutsu talent):** You can take 10 on your Ninjutsu check to execute a technique, even if you are distracted or threatened.

**Wise Ninjutsu (Requires Smart Ninjutsu talent):** As Smart Ninjutsu, but you may spend an action point to keep the better result.

**Devastating Ninjutsu (Requires Advanced Ninjutsu Talent):** If you deal damage equal or more than the damage threshold of your opponent, then you may spend an action point to make him fatigued. If he is already fatigued, then he becomes exhausted.

**Powerful Ninjutsu:** The DC against your Ninjutsu techniques increase by 2.

**Ninjutsu Insight:** You gain +3 to reflex saves against Ninjutsu.

**Ninjutsu Resilience:** You gain a +3 to fortitude saves against Ninjutsu.

**Penetrating Ninjutsu (Requires Improved Ninjutsu talent):** When you deal damage with a Ninjutsu technique, the damage threshold of the enemy counts as 5 points lower. You may take this talent multiple times, it's effect stack.

### Innate Control Talent Tree

**Elemental Insight (Element):** You can take 10 on your chakra control check to convert chakra to Hit Points for your specialized elements techniques, even if you are distracted or threatened.

**Elemental Resistance (Element):** You gain Resistance as high as your Elemental Master level for your Affinity. You can take this talent multiple times, it's effect stack, taking it 3 times makes you immune to your element.

**Elemental Understanding (Element):** You may launch an elemental attack of your Affinity that deals 3d4 + half your Elemental Master level rounded down, damage of the respective element, this ability costs 2 chakra, that can be converted to Hit Points with a successful Chakra Control DC 15 check. This is a ranged touch attack with a range of 15 ft. and a threat range of 20.

### Elemental Power

Every time this ability is gained, choose an ability from the list below. Abilities cannot be selected more than once unless specified.

**Chain Element (Requires 1 other Elemental Power):** When using a technique of your Affinity that only effects a single target, you can spend an action point to affect one additional target. Alternatively, you can spend 3 action points to affect one additional target per four character levels.

**Distant Element:** When using technique of your Affinity that has a range expressed numerically, you can spend an action point to multiply the range by 2. Alternatively, you can spend 2 action points to multiply the range by 5.

**Efficient Element (requires 1 other Elemental Power):** When using a technique of your Affinity that has chakra costs higher than 1, you may spend an action point to half that costs. Alternatively, you may spend 2 action points to divide the costs by 4.

**Empower Element (Requires 2 other Elemental Powers):** When using a technique of your Affinity that deals damage, you can spend an action point to increase the techniques damage dice by 50%. Alternatively, you can spend 3 action points to double the damage dice.

**Intense Element (Requires 3 other Elemental Power):** When using a technique of your Affinity that requires a save, you can spend an action point to increase the save DC by 3. Alternatively, you can spend 4 action points, to increase the save DC by 6.

**Persistent Element:** When using a technique of your Affinity, that has a duration greater than 1 round. You may spend an action point to double the duration. Alternatively, you can spend 4 action points to make it last 5 times as long as it would normally.

**Quicken Element:** When using a technique of your specialized element, that requires an attack or move action, you can spend an action point to execute the technique as a swift action/free action instead. Alternatively, you can spend 4 action points to execute the technique as a reaction.

**Shaped Element:** When using an elemental technique of your Affinity with an area of affect, you may spend one action point to negate a 5 ft. area within that area of effect. Alternatively, you can spend 2 action points and negate a number of 5 ft. areas equal to your Elemental Master level.
