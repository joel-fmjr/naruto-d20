# Shinobi Scribe

The shinobi scribe is a master of Fuinjutsu. With great skill, these shinobi are powerful support units that can offer a variety of special effects no other kind of technique can offer. Shinobi scribes often pair their expertise with secondary abilities (usually Ninjutsu, though not always).

## Requirements

To qualify to become a shinobi scribe, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Fuinjutsu 9 ranks, Ninjutsu, Genjutsu, Taijutsu or Chakra Control 6 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Sealweaver, Fuinjutsu Adept
*   **Special**: The character knows at least 3 sealing techniques, of which at least one must be of Rank 5.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Shinobi Scribe prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: d6
*   **Action Points**: 7 + ½ character level, rounded down.
*   **Class Skills**: Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Craft (Calligraphy, Mechanical, Visual Art, Writing) (Int), Decipher Script (Int), Demolitions (Int), Forgery (Int), Fuinjutsu (Int), Genjutsu (Cha), Knowledge (Arcane Lore, Earth and Life Sciences, History, Ninja Lore, Physical Sciences, Theology and Philosophy) (Int), Ninjutsu (Int), Profession (Wis), Read/Write Language (None), Research (Int), Search (Int), Sleight of Hand (Dex) and Spot (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Shinobi Scribe prestige class.

### Sealing Expertise

You gain a +2 bonus to all Fuinjutsu checks. Additionally, your Fuinjutsu threshold increases by 1, and you gain a +1 bonus to Learn checks when learning Fuinjutsu techniques. If you do not already have the Advanced Seal Proficiency feat, you gain it now.

### Creative Calligraphy

One of the skills most valued by a Fuinjutsu expert is to always be prepared. As long as you have ink or some other colored liquid with which you can draw shapes, you can scribe seals, and do not suffer a penalty for crafting items without a calligraphy kit. You may assemble seals with any kind of material when performing a Fuinjutsu technique, so long as the final result has the rough shape of a seal. (For example, you could use a kunai to scratch a seal into a tree, or use twigs and rocks to form a seal) Using this method incurs a -4 penalty to the Fuinjutsu check for scribing the seal.

### Focused Script

You are adept in focusing your sealing script to increase power. The save DC of your Fuinjutsu techniques increase by 1. In addition, you gain a +2 bonus to all checks when crafting chakra-based explosives and can now craft Enhanced Explosives. These items have a craft DC 5 higher than their normal counterparts, and take twice as long to craft. They cannot be used by anyone but their crafter. You can overcharge these explosives, increasing the amount of charges they can carry by two. Each of these extra charges also increases the tag’s save DC by 1.

### Trap Seal

You are able to learn the Ninjutsu no Wana technique. For each odd-numbered step of mastery you obtain in this technique, the maximum rank of the technique you can set as a trap increases by one (up to Rank 10 total). In addition, you can select one of the following Triggers to enhance your trap:

*   **Proximity Trigger**: The radius of the trap expands by 5 feet. Can be selected one additional time via the Trap Master Sealing Specialization (see below).
*   **Timed Trigger**: You can instead set the trap to explode after a set time. You can set a timer for up to 1 minute per odd-numbered Shinobi Scribe level.
*   **Chakra Trigger**: Instead of having your trap explode when approached, you can detonate it like an explosive tag. Doing so is a swift action and requires a Chakra Control check (DC 10 + trap technique’s rank). You must be aware of the seal’s location, and no further than 50 feet away.

### Sealing Specialization

Masters of Fuinjutsu understand that most times, enemies cannot be beaten by their Fuinjutsu alone, and they develop special sealing skills to synergize with their other talents. Some, however, focus their training on an even greater mastery of sealing techniques. Each time you gain this ability, you may select one of the following. In order to select one of these abilities, your effective skill threshold for the listed technique type must be at least 12.

**Chakra Control**

*   **Ink Armor**: You have harnessed the latent power of the seals you place on your body, hardening your skin. For every two seal slots you have filled, gain a +1 natural armor bonus.
*   **Homeostasis**: Once per day, you can perform Genjutsu Kai or Genjutsu Joukai on yourself, storing the effect (with its roll) into a seal taking up a seal slot on your body. The next time you are affected by a technique that can be dispelled, the seal immediately activates and releases the stored technique against it. Lasts until you take a night’s rest.

**Fuinjutsu**

*   **Seal Delivery**: Once per day, you may deliver a Fuinjutsu technique requiring a touch attack through an Advanced Seal instead, throwing it with a ranged touch attack. If it hits, it attaches itself to the target for 1 round per odd-numbered Shinobi Scribe level, affecting it with the Fuinjutsu technique, after which it falls to the ground and becomes useless. Targets are still allowed a save if the technique allows for one.
*   **Sanguinary Script**: When scribing seals, you may use your own blood to infuse the seal with increased power and resilience. When you scribe a seal for a Fuinjutsu technique, you may take 1 damage per rank of the technique. Any attempt by anyone other than the user to remove it has a 50% chance of failure. Additionally, the user can control the seal, forcibly activating or deactivating it with a chakra control check (DC 10 + technique rank) as a move action, as long as he is within 30 feet of the seal and knows where it is.

**Genjutsu**

*   **Stare Trap**: When using the Ninjutsu no Wana technique, you may instead set a trap with any Doujutsu you know. The seal becomes effective at a 30 ft. range and triggers when it is looked at.
*   **Illusory Script**: Once per day, when scribing a seal for a Fuinjutsu technique, you may also make a Genjutsu check (DC equal to the Fuinjutsu perform DC) to make the seal invisible, or change its appearance to another seal, text, image or other visual of roughly the same size. Enemies studying the seal’s location may make a Will save (DC 10 + Shinobi Scribe Level + your Charisma modifier) to disbelieve it, but must still dispel it to uncover its true form.

**Ninjutsu**

*   **Trap Master**: You may learn another Trigger for your Trap Seal, and your Trap Seal save DC penalty is reduced by 1. Can be selected twice.
*   **Regeneration**: Once per day, you may perform a medical technique on yourself and instead store it’s healing in a seal on your body, taking up one sealing slot. The next time you are reduced below 50% health, the seal triggers and immediately heals you at 50% efficiency, after which the seal disappears.
*   **Beacon Seal**: Once per day, as a full-round action, you can infuse an Advanced Seal with at least 3 chakra and turn it into a Beacon that lasts for 5 minutes, plus one minute per additional point of chakra spent (maximum 1 minute per character level). While the Beacon is active, you are aware of its general location. When you use a Teleportation technique, you may target your Beacon to teleport to it, boosting the technique’s range by 50% and doubling its capability to move through obstacles. You (and others you may carry) will appear in the square next to the Beacon.

**Taijutsu**

*   **Seal Strike**: Once per day, when performing a Fuinjutsu technique that requires a touch attack to succeed, you may instead enhance your next melee attack (via a basic attack or technique) to deliver the seal on hit. Doing so increases the perform requirement and DC for both the Fuinjutsu and attack technique (if any) by 5. If the attack misses, the use is wasted.

### Master’s Touch

Whenever you use a Fuinjutsu technique requiring a touch attack to succeed, you may spend an Action Point to enhance it. You gain a bonus equal to your Intelligence modifier to the touch attack roll, and if successful, the save DC (if any) increases by 2. The seal, once placed, cannot be removed or deactivated during the first half of its duration. A Shinobi Scribe with this ability may spend an AP when attempting to dispel a Fuinjutsu to ignore this immunity. Once per day, you may also make a melee touch attack against an opponent who carries a seal on their body (in one of their sealing slots) that you’ve previously identified. If successful, you can forcibly activate or deactivate the seal for up to one round per Shinobi Scribe level, as though their user had decided to activate them. While a seal is affected, its user cannot activate or deactivate it themselves.

### Seal Slot Mastery

You have achieved great control over the seal slots on your body, and can even transfer seals between yourself and someone else. When you fail the first Will save against a Fuinjutsu technique, your body automatically responds, allowing you a second save against the technique. Once per day, you may make a touch attack against a creature to transfer a seal from one of your seal slots onto the target or transfer a seal from one of their seal slots onto yourself. Transferred seals carry all properties they had before the transfer. Targets can save against the technique as normal, and may resist having their seal stolen with a Will save (DC 10 + one-half character level + your Intelligence modifier).

### Master Scribe

A true master of the sealing arts, you add a personal ‘dialect’ to your seals, drastically altering the composition of your sealing arrays and can scribe a superior seal resistant to removal. The DC to identify your Fuinjutsu increases by 10. If not identified, your techniques cannot be dispelled. In addition, when you perform any Fuinjutsu technique you may designate it as a Master Seal. Doing so immediately removes the Master Seal status from any other seals you may have. You are always aware of the general direction and distance of your Master Seal, and are aware of attempts to tamper with it. Finally, level checks opponents make to dispel your Master Seal have their DC increased by 2.

### Bonus Feat

Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Builder, Iron Will, Meticulous, Chakra Penetration, Chakra Restoration, Greater Chakra Penetration, Improved Chakra Pool, Insightful Reflexes, Talented Shinobi, Technique Focus, Technical Knowledge, Will over Flesh, Craft Armor Seals, Craft Sealed Items, Craft Weapon Seals, Extend Technique, Heighten Technique, Widen Technique.
