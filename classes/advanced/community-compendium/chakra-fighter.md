# Chakra Fighter

The Chakra Fighter is a shinobi who utilizes chakra control to enhance their Taijutsu, unleashing devastating physical attacks onto their foes.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a chakra fighter, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +4
*   **Skills**: Chakra Control and Taijutsu 6 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Taijutsu Adept, Combat Martial Arts and Harmony.
*   **Special**: None

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Chakra Fighter prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: d8
*   **Action Points**: 6 + ½ character level, rounded down.
*   **Class Skills**: Balance (Dex), Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Climb (Str), Jump (Str), Listen, (Wis), Profession (Wis), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Chakra Fighter prestige class.

### Chakra-Enhanced Melee

The Chakra Fighter is able to strengthen his melee attacks by focusing chakra into his hands and feet during physical combat. Every time the user makes a successful melee attack, the Chakra Fighter adds a bonus to his damage rolls equal to his Chakra Fighter levels.

### Empowered Strength

The chakra fighter has the ability to deal massive damage with a single swing by empowering his fist with chakra. At the cost of 2 chakra per die, the chakra fighter may add 1 die of appropriate size to his damage rolls. (Up to 1 die per level of Chakra Fighter) This ability is considered a full-attack action, and it stacks with the chakra fighter’s Chakra-Enhanced Melee ability.

### Chakra Explosion

Three times per day, the chakra fighter can make a slam attack that harnesses a large amount of chakra. First, the user must make a Chakra Control check (DC 15) to gather the chakra into his limb of choice. Then the user slams the ground. The attack causes an explosion that erupts in a 10-foot radius around the user. Anyone caught in the area of effect takes 10d6 damage which can be halved by a successful Reflex save (DC 10+ character’s Strength Modifier + Chakra Fighter Level). Anyone within 10 feet of the original area of effect must succeed a Reflex save of the same amount or else suffer 2d6 points of damage and fall prone. This ability is considered a fullattack action.

### Bonus Feat

Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Brawl, Chakra Penetration, Chuunin, Defensive Martial Arts, Empowered Resilience, Flawless Form, Greater Chakra Penetration, Improved Brawl, Improved Chakra Pool, Innate Control, Jounin, Power Critical, Streetfighting, Superior Combat Martial Arts.
